# List / Detail Pattern

## When To Use

- Browsing rosters, sessions, or similar collections that lead to a focused next action.

## Why It Works

- It keeps scanning simple and preserves a clear path into a deeper task.

## What To Preserve

- Fast scanability.
- One obvious transition into detail or action.
- Calm hierarchy on mobile and desktop.

## What To Avoid

- Turning the list into a dense dashboard.
- Hiding the next step behind multiple menus.
