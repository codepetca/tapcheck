# App DNA

## Primary Users

- Teachers and staff taking attendance quickly on a phone at the classroom door.
- Staff who create, import, and manage their own rosters without admin overhead.

## What Must Feel Fast

- Opening the attendance editor.
- Marking attendance with one tap.
- Finding the next student or roster action.

## What Must Feel Simple

- Creating or importing a roster.
- Starting a session.
- Understanding what to do next on any screen.

## What Should Never Happen

- Users getting lost between multiple competing actions.
- Roster ownership or auth boundaries becoming ambiguous.
- A routine attendance task feeling like admin software.

## Product Tone

- Quiet, practical, and teacher-first.
- Confident enough for daily use without feeling heavy.

## Design Tone

- Minimal, calm, mobile-first, and lightly structured.
- One primary surface, one primary action, low visual noise.
